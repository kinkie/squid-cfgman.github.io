Option Name:icp_port
Replaces:udp_port
Requires:
Default Value:ICP disabled.
Suggested Config: 


	The port number where Squid sends and receives ICP queries to
	and from neighbor caches.  The standard UDP port for ICP is 3130.

	Example:
		icp_port 3130
